<em>Third</em>: I chose the spot for the PSU mainly for the shape, but as an additional check, studying the left side of the motherboard showed the USB ports on the inside of the computer. The power source is usually in close proximity to the peripheral sockets since that's where the power cord goes.
<p></p>
Choosing the spot between the Fan and HDD came down to design. I saw 2 green clips at the base of the case side-by-side, and figured 2 HDDs could go there, but not 2 fans. That left the bottom right corner as the last spot for the fan, but also pointed the fan toward the rest of the motherboard for cooling.
